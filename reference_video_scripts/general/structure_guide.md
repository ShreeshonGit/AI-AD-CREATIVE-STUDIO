# General High-Converting Video Script Format Guide

This file acts as a structural reference for video ad scripts. It outlines how scenes are divided into visual cues and voiceovers.

**Hook Scene (0-5 seconds)**
* Visual: A high-contrast, attention-grabbing setup that visualizes the core pain point immediately. On-screen text flashes the core question.
* Voiceover: Did you know that most businesses lose up to 40% of their potential sales due to bad operational bottlenecks?

**Body Scene 1 (5-15 seconds)**
* Visual: The character struggles with the status quo, showing real frustration (e.g. staring at disorganized lists, manual rate updates, or empty sign-up forms).
* Voiceover: You spend hours manually tracking rates or generating leads, yet margins slip and conversions flatline. It feels like throwing money into a black hole.

**Body Scene 2 (15-25 seconds)**
* Visual: A clean, sleek visualization of the automated solution working. The screen transitions to smooth, stress-free dashboards.
* Voiceover: But it doesn't have to be this way. With our automated framework, everything aligns, margins stay protected, and high-quality leads flow on autopilot.

**Call to Action Scene (25-30 seconds)**
* Visual: Close-up on the final CTA screen, complete with a clean button link and limited-time offer highlights.
* Voiceover: Take control of your operations today. Click below to book your free demo and secure your exclusive 50% discount now.
